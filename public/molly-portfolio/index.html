3-Tier ABM Campaign

A fully custom account-based marketing program built from competitive research — segmented by company size, competitor platform, and messaging strategy.

The Strategy

Starting from scratch, I researched and identified the current software provider for every prospect in our target list — then segmented them into three competitive tiers based on platform type and deal size. Each tier received a fully customized email sequence with messaging tailored to their specific software pain points, switching concerns, and business size.

The campaigns ran through Pardot Engagement Studio with lead scoring built in to surface the highest-intent accounts for sales follow-up.

  • 1Tier 1 — Enterprise TargetsLargest wineries on legacy platforms. Messaging focused on modern features and scalability.
  • 2Tier 2 — Mid-MarketMid-size accounts on direct competitors. Messaging anchored to differentiation and ease of switching.
  • 3Tier 3 — Growth TargetsSmaller accounts on general-purpose platforms. Messaging focused on wine-industry specificity.
